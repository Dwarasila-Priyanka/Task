About BTS

BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed in Seoul in 2013. They are known for their meaningful lyrics, powerful performances, and massive global fan base called ARMY.

Members of BTS

RM (Kim Namjoon)
Leader and main rapper of BTS, RM is known for his intelligence, deep lyrics, and fluent English.He's a thoughtful speaker and often represents BTS in global forums and interviews.
Jin (Kim Seokjin)
The oldest member, Jin is loved for his humor, visuals, and powerful vocals.He brings warmth and balance to the group with his calm, caring nature.
Suga (Min Yoongi)
A talented rapper and producer, Suga is known for his introspective lyrics and quiet charisma.He also releases solo music under the name Agust D.
J-Hope (Jung Hoseok)
The group's main dancer and rapper, J-Hope brings vibrant energy and optimism to BTS.His stage presence and versatility make him a fan favorite.
Jimin (Park Jimin)
Known for his graceful dance and emotive voice, Jimin connects deeply with fans through his performances.He's also recognized for his kindness and dedication.
V (Kim Taehyung) - Vocalist
V's deep voice and unique charm make him stand out, both vocally and visually.He has a strong interest in art, fashion, and acting.
Jungkook (Jeon Jungkook)-Main Vocalist
The youngest member, Jungkook is the group’s main vocalist and an all-rounder.He excels in singing, dancing, and athletics, often called the "Golden Maknae."
